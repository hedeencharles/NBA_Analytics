About the Creators

This project was created by Daniel Carter, Jarrod Williams, Charles Hedeen, Rob Savage, and Franklin Truong

In this project we looked at these questions:

  • Is there a correlation between a player's birthplace and NBA success?
  • Have the total number or ratio of foreign players increased since the formation of the NBA?
  • Does the regular season win share metric correlate to playoff success?
  • Do load management techniques correlate to playoff success?

Analysis of Avg Salary overtime:

  • The average salary per year increases relatively gradually bewteen 1990-2000
  • Average salary on a decline from 2008-2013 (this correlates with the 2008 economic recession)
  • Salary spikes in 2013 before falling again
  • Salary increases at a greater rate between 2014 and 2017
